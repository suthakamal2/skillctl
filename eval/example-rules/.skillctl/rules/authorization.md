# Authorization

Deny by default; check on every request; scope tokens to least privilege.

## When this applies
Loads when the prompt mentions: authorization, permission, rbac, access control.

## Guidance
- Deny by default; check on every request; scope tokens to least privilege.
- Make the safe choice the default; document the exception when you deviate.
- Prefer the boring, well-understood option over the clever one.
